# Design Brief — Neuro Spine Pro Public Funnel

## Overview
A patient-facing acquisition funnel for Neuro Spine Pro, guiding prospective patients from initial awareness through to booking a consultation.

## Goals
- Establish trust and clinical authority quickly
- Reduce friction from landing to appointment booking
- Communicate complex spinal/neurological conditions in plain language
- Convert anonymous visitors into booked consultations

## Target Audience
- Adults 35–70 experiencing chronic back, neck, or neurological symptoms
- Caregivers researching treatment options for a family member
- Patients seeking a second opinion after a prior diagnosis

## Tone & Voice
- Calm, authoritative, reassuring
- No jargon without immediate plain-language explanation
- Empathetic — patients are often in pain or distressed

## Key Pages
1. **Hero / Landing** — condition-specific entry points (back pain, sciatica, herniated disc, etc.)
2. **About / Trust** — credentials, board certifications, outcomes data
3. **Conditions** — condition library with symptom checkers
4. **Treatment Options** — non-surgical first, surgical when indicated
5. **Patient Stories** — anonymised testimonials and outcome summaries
6. **Book a Consultation** — low-friction scheduling flow

## Constraints
- WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility minimum
- Must load under 2 s on 4G mobile (Lighthouse performance ≥ 90)
- HIPAA-aware — no PHI collected before the booking step
- Brand palette defined in `tokens.css`

## Success Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Landing → Booking conversion | ≥ 4 % |
| Bounce rate | ≤ 45 % |
| Time to book (from first visit) | ≤ 3 min |
| Accessibility score | 100 |
